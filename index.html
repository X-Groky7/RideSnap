🏠 Origin Story
It started in a mancave — the kind of place where random ideas get scribbled down and half of them never leave the whiteboard.

Ilan (Sugilan Raman) was brainstorming ideas for an app or website when a simple thought hit him: why is there no proper ride price comparison site for Southeast Asia? You want to know if Grab or inDrive is cheaper right now — but there's nowhere to just check. You'd have to open three apps, enter the same route three times, then try to remember the prices.

That gap was the seed. That night, Ilan sat down with his coding buddy Claude AI, fired up a Mac and an iPhone, and started building the thing that should've already existed. No investors. No office. No team of 20. Just a human with an idea and an AI ready to build it — inside a mancave.

RideSnap SEA was born from that one honest observation. ✊
